## üß† Instructional Design Philosophy

My approach is grounded in three core principles:

**1. Learner-Centered Design**  
Every design decision starts with understanding learner needs, prior knowledge, and goals. I use backward design, begin with clear learning outcomes, and design assessments and instruction that authentically measure those outcomes.

**2. Evidence-Based Practice**  
I ground my work in cognitive science, learning theory research, and data from pilot implementations. My designs incorporate principles of cognitive load theory, spaced repetition, retrieval practice, and multimedia learning.

**3. Ethical Technology Integration**  
As AI and educational technology evolve, I prioritize transparency, accessibility, privacy, and alignment with human values. I advocate for responsible adoption frameworks that empower rather than replace human expertise.
